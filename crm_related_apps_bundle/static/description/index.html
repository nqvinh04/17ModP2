Credit Note from CRM

Mass Update CRM Stage Pipeline

Mass Update CRM Stage Pipeline

This app will allow your sales team user to update CRM pipelines / leads / opportunity from the list view in bulk for the same team. If the user tries to select record(s) from different sales teams on the list then the system will raise a warning.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/mass_crm_pipeline_stage_update/

Timesheets on CRM Pipeline/Opportunity

Timesheets on CRM Pipeline/Opportunity

This app allows your crm team / users to fill timesheets directly in CRM pipeline form view. This will allow them to fill the timesheet for the CRM Opportunity / crm pipeline they are working on.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/crm_timesheets_odoo/

Quick Purchase Products from CRM

Quick Purchase Products from CRM

This app allows your CRM user to quickly add product lines which are going to add on CRM which further generates requests for quotation to multiple vendors.
