Bill of Material (BOM) Engineering Change Order (ECO)

Engineering Change Order of Bill of Material (BOM) in Odoo

Definition of ECO: An engineering change order (ECO) is a documentation packet that outlines the proposed change of Bill of material for Product / Items.


Main Features:

  • This app allows you to create an Engineering Change Order (ECO) of Bill of Material of MRP.
  • Bill of Material will be maintained using version information as shown.
  • Workflow of full approval process and activation of new version of Bill of Material.
  • Allow you to print a PDF report of the Engineering Change Order (ECO) of Bill of Material.
  • On activation OLD BOM will be inactive/archive and new version BOM will be activated.
  • For more details please watch below screenshots and watch the video.
