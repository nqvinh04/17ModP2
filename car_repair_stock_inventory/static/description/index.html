Car Repair App Integration with Stock Inventory / Pickings

Car Repair and Car Repair Job Orders Integration with Stock Inventory - Odoo App

This app allows your car repair team and inventory team to integrate Stock Moves and Stock Pickings of Car Repair Requests and Car Related Job Orders / Work Orders.

Main Features:

  • Integrate Car Repair Request with Stock Moves of Stock Picking by allowing your user to select a car repair request on stock move on stock picking form.
  • Integrate Job Orders with Stock Moves and Stock Pickings by allowing them to select a car related job order / work order on stock move of stock picking.
  • Inventory User(s) can select a Car Repair Request and a Job Order on Stock Pickings Form as shown.
  • Car Repair Request Form View with Stock Moves and Stock Pickings Smart Buttons as shown screenshot.
  • Job Order | Work Order Form View with Stock Moves and Stock Pickings smart buttons.
  • Your inventory team can choose car repair requests and job order on stock picking form during incoming, outgoing, internal transfer. And based on this selection stock will be shown and integrated with car repair requests and job orders of car repairs.
  • User(s) can see Stock Moves by Car Repair Requests Report under Stock Reporting Menu.
  • User(s) can see Stock Moves by Job Order Report under Stock Reporting Menu.
  • For more details about car repair module please click on this link : (https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/16.0/car_repair_maintenance_service)
  • For more details please see below screenshots and watch the video.
