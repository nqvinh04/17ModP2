Sale Contract Subscription Email Notification in Odoo

Customer Email Notification / Alert for Sales Contract Subscription with Running, Expire Soon and Expired Status

Customer Sales Contract and Subscription Notification

Only Compatible Odoo Community Edition

This app sends email notifications to customers when sales contracts get in progress, expire soon and expired.


Main Features:

  • Added reminder date in Contracts Form View for expire soon email.
  • Stage (Running) : Email Template (Contract Subscription Running) will be sent to the customer.
  • Stage (Expires Soon) : Email Template (Contract Subscription Expiring Soon) will be sent to the customer.
  • Stage (Expired) : Email Template (Contract Subscription Expired) will be sent to the customer.
  • if the Contract stage is in the Running(in progress) stage, then send email to the customer of his/her Contract is in progress.
  • if the Contract stage is in the Expires Soon(to_expired) stage, then send email to the customer of his/her Contract is going to expire soon.
  • if the Contract stage is in Expired(expired) stage, then send email to the customer of his/her Contract is expired.
  • For more details please watch the video and check below screenshots.
