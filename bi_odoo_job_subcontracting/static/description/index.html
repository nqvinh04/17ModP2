Project Job Order and Job Costing Subcontracting Odoo Apps

Project Job Order Subcontracting Odoo apps used to create subcontractor job orders, create purchase order from subcontractor jobs in construction job costing.

Project Construction Job Costing and Job Cost Sheet Odoo app used to manage project job cost and job cost sheet with material request in construction management system in Odoo This Odoo module also help to calculate progress costing and cost calculation in each Construction project job cost with job order with Material Product Purchase Requisitions it use for product purchase requisition process by employee and users. Once purchase requisition placed for required goods and material, by default it will be place from the configured location for users and employee. Project Job cost sheet calculates complete cost of the each project including material, labour and overhead cost for material it's also generate stock movement when material is consume for the process. User can able to print different reports for this job costing apps.

Demo Request Video Tutorial

Create Job Cost Sheets

You can create job cost sheet goto Job Costing > Job Costing > Job Cost Sheets.

You can add Material, Labour and Overhead details in job cost sheet.

You can also see total material cost,total labour cost,total overhead cost and total cost for job cost sheet.

Create Job Order

You can create Job Order for job costing. Click on "Create Subcontractor Job" button to create Subcontractor job order.

Subcontractor Job Order Wizard

After Clicking "Create Subcontractor Job" the wizard will open where you can assign responsible user, Job and Subcontractor.

Subcontractor View

Find 'Sub Contractor' menu under 'Job Costing'. You will find all the created subcontractor. Click on "Create Purchase Order" button to Create purchase order for the subcontractor.

Create Purchase Order Wizard

After clicking the 'Create Purchase Order' button, the wizard will open where you have to assign the Vendor.

Created Purchase Order

Purchase order will be created with the selected vendor from the wizard and purchase order line in subcontractor.

Subcontractor Job Order Portal View

You can see the subcontracting job order directly from website. Click on the 'Subcontractor Job Order' link to see the details view of Subcontractor.
