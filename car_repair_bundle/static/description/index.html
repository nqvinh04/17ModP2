Car Repair Management, Diagnosis and Job Orders

Car Repair Management, Diagnosis and Job Orders

This app allows you to manage your business/industries/workshops of car repair with features listed below and screenshots.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_maintenance_service/

Car Repair Integration Fleet App

Car Repair Integration Fleet App

This app allows you to create vehicle and vehicle services from car repair requests. This provides integration between a car repair app with fleet management (Odoo Standard Fleet App).

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_fleet_integration/

Material Purchase Requisitions for Car Repair Request

Material Purchase Requisitions for Car Repair Request

This app allows your team to create Material Requisition for Car Repair Request. Your team can request products/materials from the car repair request form and it will create material requisition using wizard.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_material_requisition/

CAR Repair Integration with Stock Inventory

CAR Repair Integration with Stock Inventory

This app allows your car repair team and inventory team to integrate Stock Moves and Stock Pickings of Car Repair Requests and Car Related Job Orders / Work Orders.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_stock_inventory/

Sales Estimate from Car Repair Request

Sales Estimate from Car Repair Request

Allow you to create Sale Estimates for Car Repair Request.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_estimate/

Job Cost Sheet for Car Repair Works/Jobs

Job Cost Sheet for Car Repair Works/Jobs

This app allows you to create a job cost sheet for car repair services so you can estimate your different jobs of car repair before giving a quote to your customer. You can prepare a job cost sheet for car repair jobs.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_job_costsheet/

Job Inspection for Car Repair Requests

Job Inspection for Car Repair Requests

This app allows you to have a job inspection feature on your car repair request for a car repair app. Your team can create job inspection requests for car repair requests.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_job_inspection/

Car Repair Request with Multi Services

Car Repair Request with Multi Services

This app is developed to handle business/industries of car repairs using Odoo. This app allows your customer to select multiple car repair services on the car repair request form on your website.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/web_car_repair_multi_service/

Purchase Order & Stock Picking with Reference of Car Repair Request

Purchase Order & Stock Picking with Reference of Car Repair Request

This app allows your team to set a car repair reference on created internal picking and purchase orders from material requisition. It also set a job order reference on created internal picking from material requisition.

For More Details: https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/17.0/car_repair_material_requisition_extend/
