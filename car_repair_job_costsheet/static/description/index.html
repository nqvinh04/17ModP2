Job Costing Sheet for Car Repair Works / Jobs

Car Repair Request App Integrate with Job Costing Sheet App

Odoo App for Job Costing Sheet for Car Repair Job Orders

This app allows you to create a job cost sheet for car repair services so you can estimate your different jobs of car repair before giving a quote to your customer. You can prepare a job cost sheet for car repair jobs.

Main Features:

  • Allow your team to set consume material, labour cost planning and overhead cost plannings on car repair request form and using it allow you to create a job cost sheet for that car repair request of a customer.
  • Basically it will allow your car team manager to plan the materials to be consumed, labours to include and overheads cost for car repair request so your team can plan and discuss with the customer about planning cost.
  • For more details about the job costing sheet you can check the dependent app(s).
    • https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/16.0/odoo_job_costing_management/
    • https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/16.0/car_repair_maintenance_service/
