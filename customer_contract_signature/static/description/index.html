Customer Signature on Contract (In Customer Portal My Account)

Sales Contract Customer Signature

Subscription Customer Signature

This app allows your customers to do signature on contracts from the website portal on my account.


Main Features:

  • Allow your customer to sign on sales contract & view sign on portal my account.
  • Customers will get email for signature requests and can do signature on contract on a website portal.
  • Once a customer signs a contract they will get a response and thank you mail from the system.
  • Customers can view signatures on the portal of your website.
  • This module is Only Compatible with Odoo Community Edition.
  • For more details please check below screenshots and watch the video.
